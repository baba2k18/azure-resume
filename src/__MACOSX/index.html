About Me

Hi, I'm Bashier Baroud, Cloud Architect at Wortell and I would like to welcome you to my Cloud Resume hosted on Azure. I have started my career at Atos back in 2010, in the role of a Business Analyst and Requirement Engineer. In this role I have served as a bridge between the technical and functional departments, helped stakeholders in identifying their needs and resolve any conflicts.

I made the move to the world of Virtualization in 2012 and started my technical endeavour in the world of Infrastructure and Application design, implementation and management. I have helped multiple companies in their digital journey, defining architectural design decisions, managing their environment, optimizing business processes, identifying issues and risks, advising which solution fits with their environment and requirements, created cost estimations, migrated several customers to the cloud, performed Health Scans and Readiness Scans on customer Azure environments and more, all with the goal to simplify complex environments and help colleagues and customer alike in making their lives easier.

In 2019 I have started working with Microsoft Azure and designing and building a Citrix environment using Azure as an IAAS platform. In 2020 I made the decision to transition to the Cloud department within Atos where I have deployed an enterprise landing zone environment for a global customer. After 11 years' working at Atos, it was time to find out how other companies did their thing, so I went from a multi-national to a much smaller and younger organization called Wortell, where I am currently working as a Cloud Solutions Architect and doing my best to help the customer in their Cloud journey.
